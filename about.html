Brandon Walowitz

About

Brandon is a designer, creative director, brand strategist, and video producer from Portland, Oregon.

Crafting Narratives That Resonate and Inspire

In the realm of digital storytelling, Brandon stands as a luminary, merging a profound understanding of video, design, and multimedia to weave stories that captivate and connect. Originating from the vibrant city of Chicago and journeying through Los Angeles and New York before settling in Portland, Oregon, Brandon's creative career has been a relentless pursuit of excellence. His work is not just about creating content; it's about crafting narratives that resonate with audiences and inspire action. With a background that spans across design, brand strategy, and video production, he's more than just a creative director – he's a storyteller at heart, a visionary who can see the big picture without losing sight of the minutiae that make a story come alive.

Creativity and Strategy

What sets Brandon apart is his unique ability to blend creative vision with strategic execution. This rare blend ensures that every project he undertakes is not only aesthetically pleasing but also strategically sound. His approach is holistic, considering every aspect of brand communication to ensure a cohesive and compelling brand story. Whether he's working directly with executives or interdisciplinary teams, his focus remains unwavering: to bring brands to life in a way that's both innovative and impactful. His portfolio, a testament to his skill and passion, showcases an array of projects that span digital, print, and video – each a distinct piece of a larger narrative puzzle.

A Beacon for Brands and Audiences Alike

Brandon's work transcends mere visual appeal; it's about creating a connection, a moment where the brand and its audience meet in a space of shared values and visions. This connection is the cornerstone of his philosophy. Through his work, brands don't just speak; they resonate. They don't just show; they reveal. And in this revealing, there's an engagement that's both profound and lasting. For those seeking not just to be seen but to be remembered, Brandon Walowitz is the go-to creative force, a maestro in the art of storytelling through design and multimedia.
